Barbecued Roast Chick on a Bun

Description

Creamy, melty, or crispy, these chicken and cheese combos will make your summer delicious. Take with a chilled drink to make all worries go away.

Ingredients

  • Chicks
  • Cheese
  • Marinade
  • Spices
  • Soy oil

Steps

  1. Wash all utensils and place the frying pan on fire.
  2. Soak the meat in the marinade for 24 hours before cookimg
  3. Add 3 liters of oil and wait for 5 minutes under high heat
  4. Blend your spices and add little water to moisturize
  5. Mix the cheese with a fraction of the blended spices and place in the oven.
  6. Add the crust of the blended spices to the cooking oil followed by the meat
  7. Allow to simmer under low heat for 8 minutes
  8. Remove the fried pieces from oil and leave to dry for 5 minutes
  9. Remove the cheese too from the oven
  10. Voila! Serve with a chilled drinka and enjoy with your family.
